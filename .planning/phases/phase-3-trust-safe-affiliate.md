# Phase 3 - Trust-Safe Affiliate Layer

## Goal
Add high-intent affiliate monetization that remains transparent and trust-safe.

## Sequencing
1. Intent-mapped partner placement model.
2. Disclosure and consent-aware attribution layer.
3. Monetization vs retention trust impact validation.

## Deliverables
- Contextual partner recommendations tied to service decision moments.
- Explicit commercial labels and consent-aware click attribution.
- Category-level affiliate funnel + retention/trust impact dashboards.

## KPI Exit Criteria
- Affiliate CTR >=3% on high-intent surfaces.
- Click-to-conversion >=5% in at least one category.
- 100% disclosure audit pass rate.
- No statistically significant retention decline vs matched control.
- Trust complaint rate for hidden sponsorship <1%.

## Risk Controls
- Pause rollout immediately on trust regression.
- Prioritize fewer high-quality partners over noisy inventory.
- Require disclosure checks in QA and analytics audits.
