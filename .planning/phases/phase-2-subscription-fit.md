# Phase 2 - Subscription Fit

## Goal
Convert proven value into subscription revenue without breaking retention.

## Sequencing
1. Premium value feature set.
2. Paywall/trial experiment framework.
3. Cohort monetization and churn reason analysis.

## Deliverables
- Premium forecasting depth, multi-vehicle workflows, and richer exports.
- Contextual paywall triggers and monthly/annual plan options.
- Trial variants and cancellation reason collection.

## KPI Exit Criteria
- Trial start rate from paywall viewers >=8%.
- Trial-to-paid >=25% baseline.
- Month-2 payer retention >=70%.
- Refund rate <4%.
- Free-tier D30 retention drop <=15% vs baseline.

## Risk Controls
- Global rollback rule if retention/trust decline under monetization pressure.
- Segment paywall timing by user value milestones.
- Maintain meaningful free utility.
